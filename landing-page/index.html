How It Works

A three-step system that keeps every ad moment authentic and seamlessly integrated.

01

Understand the Scene and User

Preprocessing builds a scene index from VideoDB, aligning transcripts to product moments with precision timing. We leverage user attributes from streaming platform data to personalize product recommendations for each viewer.

02

Invoke MCP Stack

When playback starts, Dedalus Labs MCP calls our shopping toolkit with integrations from Kroger and DoorDash to search for products, find nearby inventory in real-time, and recommend the best options to the user based on their preferences and location.

03

Overlay & Checkout

Using VideoPainter, an open source AI model, we add branded overlays directly onto products in the scene with the brand we wish to recommend. The product then surfaces in the sidebar for instant checkout or save for later.
