What we do

We turn movies into shoppable moments without breaking the story. Seamless analyzes every scene, maps products to transcripts, and lets viewers grab the exact item they saw, instantly.

Preprocessed scene index

We index scenes and product-transcript links with VideoDB and an LLM layer ahead of time.

On-frame brand overlay

Ads are branded directly on the product using Kling for photoreal overlays.

Instant sidebar checkout

Viewers can buy now or save for later without pausing the movie.

Seamless Engine

From scene to shelf in seconds.

A multimodal pipeline turns scenes into a searchable graph, then orchestrates the right product moment at the right time.

How we do it

A three-step system that keeps every ad moment authentic.

01

Understand the scene

Preprocessing builds a scene index from `videodb.ipynb`, aligning transcripts to product moments.

02

Invoke the MCP stack

When playback starts, Dedalus Labs MCP calls our shopping toolkit to find nearby inventory.

03

Overlay + checkout

We apply the Kling brand overlay, then surface the product in the sidebar for instant checkout or save.
