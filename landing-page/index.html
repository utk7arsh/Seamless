How It Works

A three-step system that keeps every ad moment authentic and seamlessly integrated.

01

Understand the Scene

Preprocessing builds a scene index from VideoDB, aligning transcripts to product moments with precision timing.

02

Invoke MCP Stack

When playback starts, Dedalus Labs MCP calls our shopping toolkit to find nearby inventory in real-time.

03

Overlay & Checkout

Apply Kling brand overlay, then surface the product in the sidebar for instant checkout or save.

Why Seamless

Advertising should respect the story and the viewer. Seamless protects the viewing experience while unlocking new revenue.

For Viewers

No forced breaks. Ads that enhance the scene, not derail it. Shop products naturally without leaving the story.

For Creators

Monetize without sacrificing tone, pacing, or authenticity. Keep your artistic vision intact.

For Brands

Premium attention in moments of real intent and relevance. Reach viewers when it matters most.
